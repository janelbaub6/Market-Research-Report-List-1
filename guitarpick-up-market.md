<p><strong>The (Guitar)Pick-Up Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Hole Pickup</li><li>Piezoelectric Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar pick-up market primarily features two types: sound hole pickups and piezoelectric pickups. Sound hole pickups, traditionally used in acoustic guitars, are placed in the sound hole and capture the vibrations of the strings and body, enhancing tonal quality. Piezoelectric pickups, commonly found in acoustic and electric guitars, utilize piezo crystals to convert string vibrations into electrical signals, often mounted under the bridge or saddles. Both types cater to different playing styles and tonal preferences in the music industry.</p></p>
