<p><p>Metalized flexible packaging encompasses several market types, each defined by their structural composition. Mono extruded structures are single-layer films that offer a balance of barrier properties and flexibility. Insulated structures incorporate layers that enhance thermal performance, making them suitable for temperature-sensitive goods. Laminated structures consist of multiple bonded layers, providing superior strength and barrier qualities. Co-extruded structures use multiple materials extruded together, combining the advantages of various polymers to achieve enhanced durability and protective characteristics in packaging applications.</p></p>
