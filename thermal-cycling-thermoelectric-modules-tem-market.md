<p><p>The Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Module (TEM) market is segmented into two main types: Single Stage Thermal Cycling TEM and Multistage Thermal Cycling TEM. Single Stage TEMs utilize a single thermoelectric element, providing basic temperature control for applications with modest thermal differences. In contrast, Multistage TEMs consist of multiple layers of thermoelectric elements, enabling greater temperature differentials and improved cooling or heating efficiency. These variations cater to diverse applications across industries such as electronics cooling, temperature stabilization, and energy harvesting.</p></p>
