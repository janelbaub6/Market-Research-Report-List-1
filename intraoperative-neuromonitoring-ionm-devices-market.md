<p><p>The intraoperative neuromonitoring (IONM) devices market comprises three main types: EEG monitoring, EMG monitoring, and EP monitoring. EEG monitoring tracks brain activity to detect potential neurological issues during surgery. EMG monitoring assesses muscle electrical activity to ensure motor pathways are intact, reducing the risk of nerve damage. EP monitoring evaluates sensory pathways by measuring the electrical responses of the brain and spinal cord to stimuli, ensuring that neural functions remain preserved throughout surgical procedures.</p></p>
