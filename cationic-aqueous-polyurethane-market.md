<p><p>The cationic aqueous polyurethane market comprises two main types: carboxylic acid type polyurethane and sulfonic acid type polyurethane. Carboxylic acid type polyurethane is characterized by its functionality derived from carboxylic groups, enhancing stability and adhesion in applications such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, sulfonic acid type polyurethane features sulfonic groups, providing superior water solubility and improved antimicrobial properties, making it ideal for textiles and other water-based applications. Both types offer versatile performance in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
