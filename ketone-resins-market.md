<p><strong>The Ketone Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ketone resins are classified into two main types based on their chemical structure and applications. Type I ketone resins are typically thermoplastic and are used in coatings, adhesives, and inks due to their excellent adhesion and durability. Type II ketone resins are thermosetting and offer enhanced heat resistance, making them suitable for electrical insulation and high-performance applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, catering to varying performance needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
