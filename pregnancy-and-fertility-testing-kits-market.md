<p><p>The pregnancy and fertility testing kits market includes various types of tests designed to detect hormones related to reproduction. LH urine tests measure luteinizing hormone levels to predict ovulation, aiding in fertility planning. FSH urine tests assess follicle-stimulating hormone levels, which help evaluate ovarian function. hCG blood tests detect human chorionic gonadotropin, confirming pregnancy through blood samples, while hCG urine tests offer a convenient method for early pregnancy detection at home. Each type serves a specific purpose in reproductive health.</p></p>
