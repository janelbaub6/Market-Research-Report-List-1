<p><p>Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) panels are categorized into two main types based on their properties and applications. Type I MDF is designed for general-purpose use, offering a smooth surface and ease of machining, making it suitable for furniture, cabinetry, and decorative applications. Type II MDF, on the other hand, is engineered for higher durability and moisture resistance, making it ideal for environments like kitchens and bathrooms. Both types serve distinct market needs, catering to varying performance requirements in construction and design.</p></p>
