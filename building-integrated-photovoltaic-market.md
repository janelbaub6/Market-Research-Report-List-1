<p><ul><li>Single Crystal Silicon</li><li>Polycrystalline Silicon</li><li>Thin Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building-Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) consist of three main market types: Single Crystal Silicon, Polycrystalline Silicon, and Thin Film. Single Crystal Silicon offers high efficiency and longevity, making it ideal for residential and commercial applications. Polycrystalline Silicon is cost-effective and suitable for larger installations but has slightly lower efficiency. Thin Film technology is lightweight and flexible, allowing for integration into various surfaces, although it typically has lower efficiency. Each type caters to different architectural and energy needs in the market.</p></p>
