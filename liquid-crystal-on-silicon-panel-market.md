<p><p>The Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) panel market features two primary designs: three-panel and one-panel systems. Three-panel designs utilize separate panels for red, green, and blue, delivering higher color accuracy and brightness, making them ideal for high-end projectors. In contrast, one-panel designs use a single panel that incorporates a color wheel or a micro-display, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution. This design caters to consumer electronics and entry-level products, balancing performance and affordability.</p></p>
