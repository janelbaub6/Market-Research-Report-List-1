<p><ul><li>Water Heated CO2 Vaporiser</li><li>Ambient Heated CO2 Vaporiser</li><li>Electrically Heated CO2 Vaporiser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 vaporizer market consists of three main types: water heated, ambient heated, and electrically heated vaporizers. Water heated CO2 vaporizers use hot water to increase the temperature of the liquid CO2, promoting vaporization. Ambient heated vaporizers rely on surrounding air temperature for heating, making them more energy-efficient but slower in performance. Electrically heated CO2 vaporizers utilize electric elements to generate heat quickly, offering precise control and rapid vaporization, ideal for industrial applications requiring consistent gas output.</p></p>
