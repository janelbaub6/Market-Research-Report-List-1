<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens (IOL) market features two main types: hydrophobic acrylic IOLs and hydrophilic acrylic IOLs. Hydrophobic acrylic IOLs are designed to repel water, providing a stable and durable option with reduced risk of opacification, making them ideal for premium vision correction. In contrast, hydrophilic acrylic IOLs absorb water, offering flexibility and a softer consistency, improving insertion ease. Both types aim to enhance visual acuity and patient outcomes post-cataract surgery, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
