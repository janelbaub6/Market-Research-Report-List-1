<p><p>Surgical thimbles are protective coverings used during medical procedures to enhance grip and precision. The market is primarily categorized into two types based on material: nitrile and latex. Nitrile thimbles are known for their chemical resistance, durability, and allergy-free properties, making them suitable for sensitive environments. Latex thimbles, on the other hand, offer excellent elasticity and comfort, but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Both types cater to diverse medical needs, balancing safety and functionality in surgical settings.</p></p>
